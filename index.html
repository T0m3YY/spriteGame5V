Controls

This is the default pose when doing nothing.

Hit 'a', to attack the enemies and don't get hit!

Use the left and right arrow to move forwards or backwards.

Click the space-bar for jumping and to dodge the enemies.

Game purpose

The aim of the game is to survive and defeat as many enemies as possible and ultimately survive with as many hearts as possible, this becomes more difficult as the levels increase

If the number of defeated enemies at the top right is equal to the required level score, a safe spot will be created. Here the game ends after 10 seconds and you can see your results.
